**Sodium**

* High sodium intake can lead to high blood pressure, which can increase the risk of heart disease and stroke.

**Total Carbohydrate**

* High carbohydrate intake can lead to weight gain and increased risk of type 2 diabetes.

**Total Sugars**

* High sugar intake can lead to weight gain, tooth decay, and increased risk of type 2 diabetes.

**Dietary Fiber**

* Low fiber intake can lead to constipation, hemorrhoids, and diverticular disease.

**Vitamin C**

* Low vitamin C intake can lead to scurvy, a disease that can cause bleeding gums, bruising, and impaired wound healing.

**Calcium**

* Low calcium intake can lead to osteoporosis, a disease that causes bones to become weak and brittle.

**Ingredients:**

* **CHOCOLATE:** Contains caffeine, which can cause insomnia, anxiety, and headaches.
* **ARTIFICIAL FLAVOURING SUBSTANCES (Vineyard peach):** Can cause allergic reactions in some people.
* **Maltitol:** A sugar alcohol that can cause bloating, gas, and diarrhea.
* **Wheat flour:** Contains gluten, which can be harmful for people with Celiac disease or gluten intolerance.
* **Hydrogenated vegetable oil:** Contains trans fats, which can increase cholesterol levels and risk of heart disease.
* **Palm oil:** Has been linked to deforestation and environmental damage.
* **Sodium:** Can contribute to high blood pressure.
